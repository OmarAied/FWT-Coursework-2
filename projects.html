PROJECTS

Recipe Recycle

Made with:

  • Python
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JS

Recipe Recycle is a website for people who have leftover food and ingredients and instead of it going to waste, they can input what they have and get recipe suggestions with any dietary requirements being accounted for. These recipes are scraped from the web through the Spoonacular API.

Gym Tracker

Made with:

  • Python
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JS

Gym Tracker is a web app for anyone who goes to the gym and wants an easy way to keep track of their progress, their food and see how to perform different exercises. You can create an account which will save all your past workouts and macros for you to look back on.
